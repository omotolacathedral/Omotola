About Us

Vision Of The Parish

To observe all the ordinances of God with the auction of Luli.

  • Reaching out to the world with the teachings of Christ.
  • Sharing God's love and compassion to the people.
  • Upholding the grace mandate "LULI".

Our mission to the Body Of Christ

Our mission to the Body of Christ is rooted in spreading His love and grace to all corners of the earth. To strengthen the bonds of our spiritual family through the teachings of Christ. To serve, uplift, and empower believers, nurturing spiritual growth and unity within our community.

Worship with us

With gladness in our heart, we welcome members and visitors from across the country to worship with us in Omotola Cathedral and witness the first-hand proof that the Grace of our Lord Jesus Christ is with us always.
